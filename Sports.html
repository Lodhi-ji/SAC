Sports Council

Fostering Fitness, Teamwork, and Excellence

About RGIPT Sports Council

The RGIPT Sports Council is the backbone of all sporting activities at the institute. It promotes a culture of fitness, sportsmanship, and teamwork by organizing a wide array of events, tournaments, and training sessions for both indoor and outdoor sports. With a vision to build leadership and instill a lifelong passion for sports, the council is dedicated to fostering talent and unity among students.

Our Events

Kridotsav

The annual intra-college sports festival where different campus blocks compete with energy and sportsmanship, celebrating the passion for sports and fostering unity.

National Sports Day

Celebrated with great enthusiasm, honoring India’s unity and diversity through flag hoisting, cultural performances, and friendly competitions.

Sports Leagues

Exciting basketball and volleyball leagues that bring fast-paced action and strategic teamwork, fostering unity, resilience, and sportsmanship throughout the year.

Annual Sports Fest – Energia

Energia is the flagship annual sports fest of RGIPT. It brings together athletes and enthusiasts from across the nation for a vibrant celebration of sportsmanship, competition, and unity.

  • Participation from top colleges across India
  • Wide range of indoor and outdoor sports
  • High-energy opening and closing ceremonies
  • Promotes sportsmanship, discipline, and fitness
