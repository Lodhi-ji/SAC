About SAC

The Student Activity Center (SAC) at RGIPT is the vibrant heart of campus life. It serves as a hub for creativity, collaboration, and leadership. SAC nurtures talent by providing students with avenues to explore cultural expression, technical innovation, sportsmanship, and social service. With a vision to empower students beyond academics, SAC bridges classroom knowledge with real-world skills. From managing large-scale fests to executing impactful social campaigns, students learn the essence of teamwork and responsibility.

SAC believes in inclusivity and diversity. Every student, irrespective of background, finds a place to shine. It is not just about organizing events, but about fostering leadership, resilience, and confidence. Students discover their passions, experiment, fail, learn, and ultimately succeed here.

Rules & Regulations

Official guidelines established to ensure the smooth, fair, and safe functioning of all student activities.

Event Approval Protocol

All proposed student activities must be submitted via the official SAC portal for approval at least 15 working days prior to the event date. No event promotion or execution may begin without written consent from the Dean of Student Affairs.

Financial Integrity

All councils must maintain a transparent and detailed record of expenditures. Budgets must be submitted for approval pre-event, and a comprehensive financial report must be filed within 7 days post-event.

Code of Conduct

All participants and organizers must adhere to the RGIPT code of conduct. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or unsafe behavior is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disciplinary action.

Use of Institute Branding

The official RGIPT and SAC logos may only be used on promotional materials after the design has been approved by the faculty convenor. Unauthorized use of institute branding is not permitted.

Venue & Resource Management

Organizers are responsible for the booking, setup, and subsequent cleaning of any venue used. Any damage to SAC resources or institute property will be billed to the organizing council.

Post-Event Reporting

A mandatory post-event report, including attendance figures, outcomes, and feedback, must be submitted to the SAC office within 10 days of the event's conclusion. This is crucial for future planning.
