Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To cultivate a socially responsible, empathetic, and proactive student community that leverages engineering knowledge, creativity, and compassion to drive inclusive development, promote well-being, and foster sustainable, empowered communities-within and beyond the campus.

Mission

  • Foster Social Responsibility: Encourage active participation in initiatives addressing societal challenges.
  • Promote Holistic Wellness: Foster mental, physical, and emotional well-being for students and local communities.
  • Empower Through Education: Bridge educational gaps by providing academic support to underprivileged children.
  • Encourage Critical Thinking: Nurture informed, socially aware future leaders through discussions and awareness events.
  • Drive Sustainability: Promote sustainable lifestyle habits and environmental stewardship.
  • Engage in Community Development: Conduct meaningful development programs in education, health, and skill development.

Our Clubs and Cells

Yog and Meditation Club (YMC)

Offers a peaceful space for students to connect through mindfulness and holistic well-being. Dedicated to enhancing mental clarity, physical vitality, and emotional strength through inclusive sessions in yog, breathwork, and meditation.

Head: Omkar Singh

Co-Heads: Taksh Agarwal, Ashwani Soni

Club Mission LiFE (CML)

Promotes environmental awareness and sustainable living. The club practices mindful utilization of resources to reduce carbon footprint, adopting healthy lifestyles, reducing waste, and conserving energy and water.

Head: Sudheer Patel

Co-Heads: Rudransh Mishra, Alok Yadav

Science Fair Club (SFC)

Focused on delivering quality educational support to children in nearby villages. The club works to bridge the learning gap by providing free, consistent, and engaging academic assistance in foundational subjects.

Head: Gaurav Srivastava

Study Circle (SC)

Aims to improve students' literary skills and public speaking confidence. The club organizes regular discussion sessions, debates, and workshops to develop communication, critical thinking, and a culture of open dialogue.

Head: Anjali Rai

Co-Heads: Shivanya Jaiswal, Shashank

Health and Wellness Club (HWC)

Dedicated to fostering physical, mental, and emotional well-being among students and the community. The club conducts health awareness programs, organizes health camps, and promotes hygiene and nutrition.

Head: Anushka Surendra Gupta

Co-Heads: Himanshu Sachdeva, Asvin Singh

Community Outreach Club (COC)

Serves as a bridge between students and society, driving meaningful initiatives that address community challenges. The club organizes awareness drives on education, health, digital literacy, and supports government initiatives.

Head: Shruti Porwal

Co-Heads: Abhishek Pal, Vedant Singh

Alumni Cell

Serves as a vital link between former students and the institute's ongoing social initiatives. It fosters long-term engagement with alumni who are passionate about giving back to society and mentoring current students.

Head: Utkarsh Jaiswal

Professional Development Cell (PDC)

Responsible for establishing strategic partnerships with government bodies, NGOs, and CSR wings. This cell acts as the external liaison, ensuring initiatives receive the support and resources they need.

Head: Sonal

Design and Editorial Cell (DEC)

The creative and communication backbone of the council. This cell is responsible for visually and verbally presenting the council's work through reports, newsletters, social media graphics, and promotional materials.

Head: Naman Patel

Social Media and Publicity Cell (SMP)

Plays a key role in creating awareness and building engagement for the council's initiatives. It amplifies the impact of social service work through strategic communication, event coverage, and social media management.

Head: Deboshruti Mondal

Key Events & Initiatives (2024-25)

Primary Teaching Initiative

Every weekend, volunteers engage with students at a government primary school, teaching basics of math and language, blended with interactive play to foster learning and empathy.

Blood Donation Camp

Organized in collaboration with AIIMS Raebareli, this camp successfully collected around 60 units of blood, raising awareness about the critical importance of donation.

Swachhata Pakhwada

A multi-faceted cleanliness drive supported by ONGC, including cleaning campaigns, awareness workshops, competitions, and sanitary pad distribution in nearby villages.

Science Exhibition

Around 200 students from 6 different schools showcased their innovative projects, fostering leadership, collaboration, and a spirit of scientific inquiry.

Ganesh Chaturthi Utsav

A 10-day long celebration that brought the entire student community together, fostering a sense of unity, devotion, and cultural belonging.

Krishna Janmashtami Utsav

A vibrant celebration that highlighted the significance of rituals and religious practices, fostering a sense of community and unity among all students.
