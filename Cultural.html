Student Cultural Council

Nurturing Creativity, Talent, and Cultural Exchange

Our Vision

To nurture creativity, talent, and cultural awareness among students by providing a platform for artistic expression, inclusivity, and cultural exchange. The council aims to preserve traditions while embracing modernity through diverse events and initiatives.

Kaltarang: The Annual Cultural Fest

Kaltarang is a vibrant celebration of art, talent, and youthful spirit, bringing together every shade of culture under one roof through competitions, celebrity performances, and workshops.

Drama Club (Damru)

From gripping stage plays and bold nukkad nataks to expressive mime and solo acts, our performances bring powerful stories to life, often shining a light on important social issues. Our mission is to nurture creativity and passion through theatre workshops, intra-college competitions, and meaningful street plays.

SPIC MACAY Club

SPIC MACAY is a voluntary, non-political, nationwide movement to inspire young minds to cherish and preserve the rich cultural heritage of India. We provide students with an opportunity to experience the profound beauty of Indian traditions through classical music, dance, yoga, and cinema.

Head: Vishesh Gautam

Co-Head: Anish Kalra

Film and Media Club (FMC)

A student-led community that celebrates creativity, storytelling, and the art of visual expression through photography, filmmaking, editing, and design. Through workshops, screenings, and competitions, FMC fosters an environment where ideas come alive.
