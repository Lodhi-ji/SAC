Science & Technology Council

Fostering Innovation and Collaboration at RGIPT

About the Council

The Science and Technology Council of RGIPT serves as a hub for innovation, critical thinking, and tech-driven activities. Our primary aim is to foster curiosity and experimentation among students, build a collaborative community for problem-solving, and support both technical learning and entrepreneurial ideas. We empower students to delve into technical and scientific domains, fostering a spirit of innovation to solve real-world challenges. This is achieved by providing meaningful exposure through seminars and conferences, and cultivating practical skills through hands-on workshops and interactive sessions.

About E-CELL RGIPT

Fostering Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Who We Are
The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of RGIPT is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. We serve as a platform for nurturing innovative ideas and transforming them into successful ventures.

Our Mission
Our mission is to foster an entrepreneurial mindset and provide necessary resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to aspiring entrepreneurs at RGIPT.

Tinkering Club

The Tinkering Club is the heart of innovation at RGIPT, offering students a unique platform to transform ideas into reality through hands-on learning and creative exploration in robotics, drones, and mechanics.

Key Events (2024-25)

IoT Coding & Product Development Workshop

Dive into IoT with practical coding sessions and product design. Gain skills to create smart, connected solutions from concept to prototype.

Hydraulic Arm Challenge – Urjotsav 2K24

This competition tests creativity and engineering skills by challenging participants to design and build a hydraulic-powered arm to perform a series of tasks.

Line Follower Bot Challenge – Urjotsav 2K24

Participants create autonomous robots to navigate a line track from start to finish, passing through checkpoints as quickly as possible.

Line Maze Solver Challenge – Urjotsav 2K24

This challenge tests skills in designing and programming autonomous robots to navigate a maze from the start point to the exit in the shortest time.

RC Boat Competition – Urjotsav 2K24

An exciting challenge where teams design manually operated RC boats to navigate a maze. Speed, accuracy, and problem-solving are key.

Water Rocket Competition – Urjotsav 2K24

Design and build a water rocket that maximizes air time and distance. The team with the highest combined score for range and air time wins.

RC Wall Maze Solver – Urjotsav 2K24

Guide your robot through a wall-filled maze from START to FINISH. Speed, precision, and smart navigation are the keys to victory.
